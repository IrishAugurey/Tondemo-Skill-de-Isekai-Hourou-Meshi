Tsuyoshi Mukouda

Character Description

Tsuyoshi Mukouda is the main protagonist of the anime and light novel series "Campfire Cooking in Another World with My Absurd Skill" (Tondemo Skill de Isekai Hourou Meshi). After being accidentally summoned to a fantasy world, he quickly realizes he's not cut out for fighting but possesses a unique skill called "Online Grocery". Using this, he purchases modern Japanese ingredients and cookware to prepare delicious meals, which earn him powerful allies and a peaceful yet adventurous life.

Unique Skill – Online Grocery:

Mukouda’s special ability lets him order food and supplies from modern Japan, even while living in a fantasy world.

Famous for His Cooking:

His meals are so delicious that even legendary creatures, like the mythical wolf Fel, become his loyal companions.

He Enjoys a Good Drink — but Only with Food:

Mukouda isn’t a heavy drinker, but he loves pairing beer or sake with his meals. With his Online Grocery skill, he brings quality Japanese alcohol into the fantasy world and treats it like a culinary treasure.
